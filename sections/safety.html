Safety & Compliance

Safety Is Not Optional — It's Our Standard

Every driver, every truck, every route operates under a culture of safety-first at CPARS Transportation.

Hours of Service (HOS)

All drivers follow FMCSA Hours of Service regulations strictly. No pressure to push past safe driving limits — ever.

Pre & Post-Trip Inspections

Every truck is inspected before and after every trip. Any issue gets flagged and fixed before that truck moves again.

Drug & Alcohol Testing

All CPARS drivers are enrolled in a DOT-compliant drug and alcohol testing program — pre-employment and random.

Ongoing Driver Training

Safety training doesn't stop at hiring. Our drivers receive regular updates on regulations, road safety, and best practices.

DOT Compliance

We maintain full compliance with all Department of Transportation requirements including registrations, permits, and filings.

Cargo Insurance

Every load is covered. We carry full cargo insurance so your freight is protected from pickup to delivery.
